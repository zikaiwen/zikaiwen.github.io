Four common online risk scenarios identified by Freed et al., where over 25% of parent and teen participants reported encountering these risks:

  1. Harassment: Claiming to know the target’s IP address to threaten an attack.
  2. Sexual Violence: Unwanted and inappropriate sexual advances or content sent via digital platforms.
  3. Coercive Control and Stalking: Persistent monitoring and controlling behavior online.
  4. Unsafe and Illegal Behaviors: Engaging in activities that endanger someone’s safety or are illegal, such as sharing sensitive personal information to threaten or harm an individual.
